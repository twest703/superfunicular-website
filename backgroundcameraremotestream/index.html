Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between FREE and PRO?

FREE version (ad-supported, limited features): Screen-off video recording only - perfect for basic recording needs.

PRO version (one-time purchase, full-featured, no ads): Unlocks the revolutionary screen-off YouTube streaming, remote web console for local recording, stream from files, playlists, and all advanced features. We never sell your data in either version.

Where are my recordings stored?

All recordings are stored locally on your Android device in the DCIM folder or a custom location you choose. Nothing is uploaded to cloud servers unless you explicitly stream to YouTube.

Can I access the camera remotely from outside my WiFi network?

No, the remote web console only works on your local WiFi network for security reasons, and it controls local recording only (not YouTube streaming). This prevents unauthorized internet access to your camera.

Does the app work with screen off?

Yes! This is a key feature. Background recording works perfectly with the screen off to save battery and enable discreet recording.

How is my privacy protected?

The app collects ZERO personal data. No analytics, no tracking, no telemetry. All recordings stay on your device. The remote console is local network only. We are GDPR and CCPA compliant.

What's the difference between this and other camera apps?

Unique features: (1) Screen-off recording, (2) Remote web control without installing apps, (3) YouTube Live streaming from files, (4) Playlist support, (5) Complete privacy with local-only storage, (6) Professional adaptive bitrate streaming.

Can I use this as a security camera?

Absolutely! That's one of the primary use cases. Place an old Android phone, start recording, and access remotely via web browser. Much cheaper than buying a $200-500 security camera.

Does it require root access?

No, the app works on all standard Android devices without root.

What video quality can I record?

The app supports 14 resolution presets per camera, ranging from 480p to 1080p depending on your device's camera capabilities.

How do I change the remote console password?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Authentication Settings. The default password is admin/camera123, which you should change for security.

Can I stream to YouTube without Google Play Services?

No, YouTube streaming requires Google Play Services for OAuth authentication. However, basic recording and remote console for local recording work without it.

How long can I record for?

Recording duration depends on your device's storage and battery. The app includes auto-stop at low battery/storage. You can set custom duration limits (30s to unlimited).
