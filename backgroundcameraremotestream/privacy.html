1. Introduction

Welcome to Digi Nomad's Background Camera Remote Youtube Streamer ("we," "our," or "the App"), published on Google Play Store as "Background Camera RemoteStream". This Privacy Policy explains how the app collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our Android application.

Digi Nomad's Background Camera Remote Youtube Streamer is a camera recording and remote control application that allows you to record video and photos from your Android device, manage recordings locally, stream to YouTube Live, and control your device remotely via a local network web interface.

Your privacy is important to us. This app is designed with privacy in mind—all recordings are stored locally on your device, and we do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on external servers.

By using Digi Nomad's Background Camera Remote Youtube Streamer, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Data Collected by the App

Digi Nomad's Background Camera Remote Youtube Streamer requires access to certain device features and data to function properly:

Camera Access (CAMERA Permission)

  • What we collect: Video recordings and photos captured by your device's front or back camera
  • Why we need it: To enable background camera recording, photo capture, and video streaming functionality
  • How we use it: All camera data is processed and stored locally on your device. We do not upload camera data to external servers unless you explicitly initiate a YouTube Live stream
  • Storage location: Device local storage only (app-specific directory or user-selected folders)

Microphone Access (RECORD_AUDIO Permission)

  • What we collect: Audio captured by your device's microphone
  • Why we need it: To record audio alongside video recordings
  • How we use it: Audio data is embedded in video files and stored locally on your device. Audio is only transmitted during YouTube Live streaming when you explicitly start a stream
  • Storage location: Device local storage only (embedded in video files)

Storage Access (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE)

  • What we collect: Access to read and write media files on your device
  • Why we need it: To save recorded videos and photos, access existing media files for streaming, and allow you to manage your recordings
  • How we use it: We read and write files only in directories you authorize or select. We do not access or collect data from other apps or unrelated files
  • Storage location: Device local storage only

Network Access (INTERNET, ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE, ACCESS_WIFI_STATE)

  • What we collect: Local network information (your device's local IP address and WiFi status)
  • Why we need it: To enable the local network web interface for remote control and YouTube Live streaming functionality
  • How we use it: Network access is used only for:
    • Running a local web server accessible only on your WiFi network (port 8080)
    • Connecting to YouTube Live API for streaming (only when you explicitly start a stream)
    • We do not collect or transmit analytics, tracking data, or personal information over the internet

2.2 Data We Do NOT Collect

Digi Nomad's Background Camera Remote Youtube Streamer does NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, MAC address, advertising ID)
  • Usage analytics or app behavior data
  • Location data (GPS coordinates)
  • Contact lists or phonebook data
  • Browsing history or other app data
  • Biometric data (fingerprints, facial recognition data)

App Versions:

  • PRO Version (Paid): No analytics, no tracking, no advertising libraries whatsoever
  • FREE Version (Ad-Supported): Contains AdMob advertising SDK for displaying ads, but no user tracking or data collection beyond what's required for ad serving

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Local Recording and Storage

  • All video recordings and photos are saved to your device's local storage
  • You have full control over your recordings—you can view, delete, or share them at any time
  • We do not have access to your recordings; they remain on your device

3.2 Remote Web Interface (Local Network Only)

  • The app runs a local web server on port 8080 accessible only on your WiFi network
  • This allows you to control the camera and view recordings from a web browser on the same network
  • Authentication required: The web interface requires a username and password (default: admin/camera123)
  • Security: The web interface is only accessible on your local network—it is not exposed to the internet
  • No data from the web interface is transmitted outside your local network

3.3 YouTube Live Streaming (Optional)

When you choose to use the YouTube Live streaming feature:

  • Authentication: You authorize the app to access your YouTube account using Google OAuth 2.0
  • Google account data: We request access to your YouTube channel name and the ability to create and manage live streams on your behalf
  • Video transmission: Video and audio data are transmitted directly to YouTube's servers via RTMP protocol
  • Data retention: YouTube's privacy policy governs how your stream data is stored and used. We recommend reviewing YouTube's Privacy Policy
  • Third-party access: Only you and YouTube have access to stream data. We do not store copies of streamed content on external servers

You can disconnect YouTube access at any time by:

  1. Opening the app's Settings
  2. Navigating to "YouTube Stream from File" settings
  3. Clicking "Clear Authorization" to revoke access

You can also revoke access via your Google Account settings at https://myaccount.google.com/permissions

3.4 Google Play Services

Digi Nomad's Background Camera Remote Youtube Streamer uses Google Play Services for:

  • YouTube API authentication (OAuth 2.0)
  • YouTube Live streaming functionality

Google Play Services is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. We do not have control over how Google collects or processes data through Play Services.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage Only

  • All recordings are stored locally on your device in app-specific directories or user-selected folders
  • We do not upload, sync, or back up your recordings to cloud servers
  • Your recordings remain on your device until you manually delete them

4.2 Security Measures

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data:

  • Authentication: The remote web interface requires password authentication (Basic Auth)
  • Local network only: Remote access is restricted to your local WiFi network (not accessible from the internet)
  • Encrypted credentials: YouTube OAuth tokens are stored securely using Android's encrypted storage mechanisms
  • Foreground services: Camera and recording services use Android foreground services with user-visible notifications, ensuring transparency

4.3 Data Retention

  • Recordings: Stored on your device indefinitely until you manually delete them
  • YouTube credentials: OAuth tokens are stored until you revoke access via Settings
  • Web interface credentials: Stored in app preferences until you change them or uninstall the app

4.4 Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

  • Recordings: Delete individual files via the app's "View Files" feature or your device's file manager
  • YouTube access: Revoke authorization in Settings → YouTube Stream from File → Clear Authorization
  • All app data: Uninstall the app to remove all app data, settings, and credentials from your device

Note: Recordings saved to shared device storage (e.g., DCIM folder) will persist after uninstall unless manually deleted.

5. Third-Party Services

Digi Nomad's Background Camera Remote Youtube Streamer integrates with the following third-party services:

5.1 YouTube API Services

  • Purpose: Enable YouTube Live streaming functionality
  • Data shared: Video/audio stream data, YouTube channel information
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Terms of Service: https://www.youtube.com/t/terms

By using YouTube Live streaming features, you agree to be bound by the YouTube Terms of Service and Google Privacy Policy.

5.2 RootEncoder Library

  • Purpose: Video encoding and RTMP streaming to YouTube
  • Data processing: Video/audio encoding happens locally on your device

Digi Nomad's Background Camera Remote Youtube Streamer requires the following Android permissions:
