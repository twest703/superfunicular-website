Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I use multiple promo codes?

A: No, you can only use one promo code per subscription. Once a code is applied, it cannot be changed or combined with other offers.

Q: What if my promo code doesn't work?

A: Check these common issues:

  • Code may have expired (check the expiration date)
  • Code may have reached its usage limit
  • You may have already used a promo code on your account
  • Code may be region-specific or for new users only
  • Double-check spelling (though codes are case-insensitive)

Q: Do promo codes work with the free trial?

A: Yes! Promo codes often extend the free trial period (e.g., 30 days instead of 14) or provide discounted pricing after the trial ends.

Q: Are promo codes safe to use?

A: Absolutely! Promo codes are redeemed through Google Play's official system - the same secure platform you use for all app purchases. We never see your payment information.

Q: Can I share my promo code with friends?

A: It depends on the code! Some codes (like influencer codes) are designed to be shared publicly. Others (like win-back codes) are for single-use only. Check the terms when you receive the code.

Q: What's the difference between a promo code and the free trial?

A: The free trial (14 days) is available to everyone by tapping "Try Free for 14 Days." Promo codes offer special benefits like:

  • Extended trials (e.g., 30 or 60 days instead of 14)
  • Discounted pricing (e.g., $4.99/year instead of $9.99)
  • Free months (e.g., 3 months free, then regular price)
  • Lifetime discounts (discount applies to all future renewals)

Q: I redeemed a code but Pro features aren't unlocking. What should I do?

A: Try these steps:

  • Force close the app and reopen it
  • Check Google Play Store ‚Üí My Apps ‚Üí Subscriptions to verify the subscription is active
  • Wait 1-2 minutes for Google Play to sync with the app
  • If still not working, contact support at dev@superfunicular.com

Q: Where can I see my active promo code benefits?

A: Open the Google Play Store app ‚Üí Menu ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí Background Camera RemoteStream. You'll see your subscription details, including any promotional pricing or extended trial periods.
